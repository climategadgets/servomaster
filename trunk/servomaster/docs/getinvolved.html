How to Get Involved
Every volunteer project obtains its strength from the people involved in it. We invite you to participate as much or as little as you choose. The roles and responsibilities that people can assume in the project are based merit. Everybody's input matters!

There are a variety of ways to participate. Regardless of how you choose to participate, we suggest you join some or all of our mailing lists.

Discuss it With Us
Not everything can be taken for a face value. Some things are more complicated than they seem, some are less complicated. People make mistakes, it's quite possible we've made some.

Tell us about it.

Give It a Try
Hardware is cheap, but the console will work even without a hardware. If you are here, it means that you can do it, and you need it.
Give us feedback
Some assumptions that we have made may be wrong, or not generic enough, or not specific enough. Hardware may be quirky. Operating system details may differ. Phase of the moon changes. All in all, this is a project originated by a software engineer, and it is known they're having delusions of grandeur all the time.

Or maybe this project gave you the answers to the questions you were seeking in vain.

Or we made a mistake that is trivial from your point of view.

Whatever it is, make your voice heard.

Donate Hardware
Hardware is cheap.
Software is expensive.
Software development is outrageously expensive.
We know how to do it, and we've done it, repeatedly.

Donate hardware, and get the working drivers available to all your customers in a few days.
And the name of your company gets into the Hall Of Fame.
And your customers will know you care about them.

Donate Code
Code donations are just as important as hardware donations. It doesn't really matter if you provide the hardware or the driver for it - well, in latter case you've saved us some time.
Donate Your Wishes
Tell us what you always wanted to have but never could - your wish may come true. Some people have requirements that others can't even think about but find them useful when they become available.

If you have a wish and want to fullfill it, you might have just come to a right place.
